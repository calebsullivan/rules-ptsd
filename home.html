Assess yourself: PTSD diagnosis system

A study on matching patient behavioral patterns using Rete-based rules.

This tool requests some information about a patient and uses common markers to determine at what level of confidence the patient displays signs. This is a computer science demonstration and will likely be inaccurate, only a professional can make a final diagnosis at high confidence.

No information will be transmitted, all processing will take place on the local machine in active memory only and will be destroyed when closing this window.
